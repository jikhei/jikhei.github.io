## Research interests

My research interests include:
1. **Digital humanities**
- Modeling linguistic features in texts, with a special interest in automated information extraction and visualization of narrative texts;
2. **Language technology**: 
- Leveraging NLP methodology to investigate human language processing and production, previously focus on dependency grammar and information theory; 
- Exploring (L)LMs' language capacity in following humans' instructions and acquiring real-world knowledge;
