## Research interests

My research interests include:
- **Computational humanities**: Modeling linguistic features in texts, with a special interest in automated information extraction and visualization of narrative texts, and
- **Language technology**: Exploring (L)LMs' language capacity in following humans' instructions and acquiring real-world knowledge.
